# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome newcomers
- Focus on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to luiz@flylabs.fun. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
